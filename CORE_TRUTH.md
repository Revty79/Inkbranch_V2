InkBranch v3 — Core Truth
What InkBranch Is

InkBranch is an AI-powered interactive storytelling engine.

The author creates the true story spine.
The reader experiences a personalized path through that spine.
The AI builds the story between them.

The author writes the spine.
The reader lives the variation.
The AI builds the path.

What InkBranch Is NOT
Not a general writing tool
Not a generic AI story generator
Not a worldbuilding app
Not a sandbox where the reader can break the story

If a feature does not improve interactive storytelling, it does not belong.

Core Product Rule

Stable spine. Flexible experience.

The author controls:
major events
canon
tone
outcomes that must happen
The reader controls:
choices
actions
relationships
how events are experienced
The AI controls:
scene generation
continuity
adapting the story to the reader
preserving the author’s intent
Non-Negotiable Design Rule

Readers are free inside scenes.
Authors control the arc.

Guardrail Principle

The story must adapt without breaking.

When reader input conflicts with author intent:

do NOT hard reject unless necessary
bend, redirect, or reinterpret the input
allow emotional or local impact
preserve required story outcomes

Scope Rule for v3

Before building anything, ask:

Does this directly improve the interactive storytelling engine?

If no → do not build it.
